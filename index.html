Hello,

I am Matilde Piccoli and work between Technology and Visual Arts.
I hold a Master's in Electronic and Electrical Engineering at Imperial College London and I am a freelance Visual Communicator. My expertise lies in Data Analysis, Creative Storytelling, and Artificial Intelligence.

In a Nutshell

I am eager to get involved in a dynamic context that values a diverse background, as I have always found that broadening one's range of experiences can be key asset even in one's field-specific work.

Back at university, I was committed to pursuing a Master's in Engineering in parallel with a drawing practice. I have collected different reserch and world-leading industry job experiences in Electronics. Following my graduation, I have also dedicated some time to experiment artistic collaborations around the world and further professional experiences in Illustration and Visual Communication.

Now, I am attempting to merge all I have learnt. I am combining on one side my visual techniques and storytelling, and, on the other, the data manipulation abilities and tech-world insight to analyze and communicate on critical subjects such as the social impact of technology and the environmental transition.
