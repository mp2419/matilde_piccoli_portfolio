Hello,

I am Matilde Piccoli and work between Technology and Visual Arts.
I hold a Master's in Electronic and Electrical Engineering at Imperial College London and I am a freelance Visual Communicator. My expertise lies in Data Analysis, Creative Storytelling, and Artificial Intelligence.

In a Nutshell

I am eager to contribute to a dynamic environment that values diverse perspectives, as I firmly believe that a broad range of experiences enriches field-specific expertise and fosters innovative solutions.

During university, I was committed to pursuing a Master's in Engineering while simultaneously developing my drawing practice. I gained diverse research and world-leading industry experiences in Electronics. Following graduation, I also dedicated time to experimenting with artistic collaborations worldwide and furthering my professional expertise in Illustration and Visual Communication.

Now, I am striving to merge everything I have learned. I am combining my visual techniques and storytelling skills with my data manipulation abilities and insights into the tech world to analyze and communicate critical subjects, such as the social impact of technology and the environmental transition.
