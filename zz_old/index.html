Hellooooooo,

I am Matilde Piccoli and work between Technology and Visual Arts.
I hold a Master's in Electronic and Electrical Engineering at Imperial College London and I am a freelance Visual Communicator. My expertise lies in Data Analysis, Creative Storytelling, and Artificial Intelligence.

I am always looking for opportunities for collaboration, with a particular interest in social and environmental causes.

In a nutshell

I am eager to get involved in a research context that values a diverse range of experiences, as I have always found that broadening one's background can only benefit one's technical and field-specific work.

Back at university, I was committed to pursuing a Master's in Engineering in parallel with a drawing practice. Following my graduation, I have dedicated some time to artistic projects and further formative experiences in Illustration and Visual Communication.

I am currently bridging the gap by combining my artistic visual storytelling and scientific data manipulation abilities to analyze and communicate on critical subjects such as the social impact of technology and the environmental transition.
