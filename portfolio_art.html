With my pieces of work, I aim to communicate the intimate through the unexplicit of the visual arts.
I see the arts as an individual experience inside the collective unconscious.

I believe that any art coexists with the other ones. It is through the study of Literature and Philosophy, and my experiences in Music and Theatrical performances that my passion and commitment to the Visual Arts were born.
I particularly like to focus on the human figure and interiority by experimenting with multi-media drawing.
